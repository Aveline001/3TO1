Too much and it raises blood pressure, spikes anxiety, and crashes your mood. The window that actually works is narrower than most people think.

What the research says.
A peer-reviewed study in the European Journal of Nutrition (Hearris et al.) tested caffeine across a full cognitive assessment battery.
